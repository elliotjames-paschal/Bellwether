Bell Model Roadmap

The goal: a model that takes a research question and produces publication-quality empirical analysis—methodology, data, statistical tests, robustness checks, and written findings.

  • Live Automated data collection from Polymarket & Kalshi
  • Live Market classification pipeline (15 political categories)
  • Live Brier score calculation & calibration analysis
  • Building LaTeX paper generation from pipeline outputs
  • Building Natural language → research design translation
  • Planned Dynamic SQL query generation from research questions
  • Planned Automated robustness checking & sensitivity analysis
  • Planned Fine-tuned Bell 2.0 on political science methodology
